Artist Land
We are a team who want to help connecting between artists and customers. So, we design this app mainly for freelance digital artist who are searching for customers and vice versa. We hope that we can help you to find your desired artist through this app and find your customers who are willing to pay for your worthy arts. We welcome any feedbacks.
Our App
Our app will help you find your desired artist and provide a great user experience. You can find the artist through search bar. Before you contact the artist, you might have an account for our app. You can also add your banking accounts so that you can pay the artist online.
